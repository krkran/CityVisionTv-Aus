Common Questions
How do I get featured on Hamro Story?+
Send us a message using the form with your name, a bit about yourself and why you would like to be on the show. We schedule guests for each last Saturday of the month.
Can I submit a news story or tip?+
Yes — select "Story tip or news submission" from the dropdown and tell us what the story is. Include as much detail as you can and we will follow up.
How do I advertise with City Vision TV?+
We offer advertising across our Facebook, YouTube and website. Select "Advertising & sponsorship" in the form and tell us about your business and what you are looking for.
Can I list a community event?+
Absolutely. Select "Community event listing" and provide the event name, date, time, location and a short description. We will add it to the site.
How do I appear on the Kurakani podcast?+
Select "Kurakani Podcast enquiry" and tell us your story — your background, what you would like to talk about, and your connection to the Nepali diaspora.
